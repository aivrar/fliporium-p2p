Stats refresh when you reload the page. No JavaScript ping-back, no fingerprinting, no per-visit tracking. Updated in near real time from our Headscale control server.

What each number means

Downloads
How many times fliporium.exe has been downloaded from this site. Counted server-side by our nginx access log. A retry counts once per unique IP per day.
Online right now
How many devices are connected to the Fliporium tailnet at this moment. A device is “online” if its WireGuard keepalive has fired in the last 5 minutes.
Total registered
How many unique devices have ever joined the tailnet. Includes devices that may have stopped using Fliporium and gotten cleaned up by our nightly stale-node sweep (we delete entries inactive for >24h).

What this page doesn't do

  • It does not log which IP visited which stat page.
  • It does not show per-device data of any kind.
  • It does not store cookies.
  • It does not load any third-party scripts.
